Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Data Collection

HRVSpark does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All health data stays on your devices.

HealthKit

HRVSpark reads Heart Rate Variability (HRV) data from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This data is used solely to display your HRV trends on your watch face and in the companion iPhone app. HRV data is never shared with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

Data Storage

Your HRV data is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's secure App Group container. Data syncs between your iPhone and watch via Apple's WatchConnectivity framework — a direct, encrypted, device-to-device connection.

In-App Purchases

HRVSpark uses Apple's StoreKit for the optional Pro upgrade. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. HRVSpark does not process or store any payment information.

Analytics & Tracking

HRVSpark does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, crash reporting services, or advertising frameworks. There is no telemetry of any kind.

Third-Party Services

HRVSpark does not use any third-party services. The app communicates only with Apple HealthKit and Apple's StoreKit — both on-device Apple frameworks.

Contact
